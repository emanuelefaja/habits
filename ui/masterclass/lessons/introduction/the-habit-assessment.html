What Your Assessment Reveals

This simple assessment gives you a starting point for understanding your current relationship with habits. As you progress through this course, you'll develop a much deeper understanding of your habit patterns.

Awareness

Your ability to notice your existing habits is the foundation of all habit change. Without awareness, change is nearly impossible.

Consistency

Your capacity to maintain new behaviors is what separates temporary changes from lasting transformation.

Satisfaction

Finding joy in the process, not just the outcome, is crucial for long-term habit maintenance.

Obstacle Recognition

Your ability to identify what prevents habit formation allows you to design more effective solutions.

In the coming lessons, we'll build on this foundation with practical strategies for habit design and implementation that work with your unique strengths and challenges.
